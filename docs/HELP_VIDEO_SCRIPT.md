## VIDEO 3: Career Canvas Deep Dive (4 min)

### Title: "Building Your Career Canvas - Define Your Value Proposition"

### Script Outline:

**[Introduction - 30 sec]**
"Your Career Canvas is a 9-section framework that captures who you are professionally. Think of it as your career DNA - it guides everything from your resume to interview answers."

**[The 9 Sections - 2.5 min]**

*Section 1: Who Helps You Succeed*
"List the people, teams, or resources that enable your best work. Example: 'Cross-functional teams, mentors with industry experience, supportive managers.'"

*Section 2: Activities You Love*
"What work energizes you? Example: 'Solving complex technical problems, mentoring junior developers, building user-facing features.'"

*Section 3: Value You Create*
"What outcomes do you deliver? Example: 'Increased revenue through optimized systems, improved team productivity, reduced customer churn.'"

*Section 4: Interactions You Prefer*
"How do you like to work with others? Example: 'Collaborative brainstorming, async communication, small focused teams.'"

*Section 5: How to Convince You*
"What motivates your decisions? Example: 'Data-driven arguments, clear vision, growth opportunities, work-life balance.'"

*Section 6: Skills You Bring*
"Your core competencies. Example: 'Python, AWS, system design, technical leadership, agile methodologies.'"

*Section 7: What Motivates You*
"Your internal drivers. Example: 'Building impactful products, continuous learning, recognition for excellence.'"

*Section 8: What You Sacrifice*
"Trade-offs you're willing to make. Example: 'Will relocate for right opportunity, prefer equity over higher base salary.'"

*Section 9: Outcomes You Want*
"Your career objectives. Example: 'Senior leadership role in 3 years, work at innovative company, six-figure salary.'"

**[Features Demo - 45 sec]**
- Show auto-save indicator
- Demonstrate progress percentage
- Preview "Business View" format
- Show how to create multiple canvases

**[Pro Tips - 15 sec]**
"Be specific. Generic answers like 'I'm a hard worker' won't help. Use real examples from your experience."

## VIDEO 4: 90-Day Plan Builder (4 min)

### Title: "Creating Your 90-Day Career Action Plan"

### Script Outline:

**[Why 90 Days? - 30 sec]**
"Research shows 90 days is the optimal planning horizon - long enough to achieve meaningful goals, short enough to stay focused. Our 12-week structure breaks this into manageable weekly milestones."

**[Creating Your First Plan - 60 sec]**
1. Click "Create New Plan"
2. Enter plan title (e.g., "Q1 2025 Job Search")
3. Set start date
4. Plan auto-calculates 12 weeks

**[Adding Milestones - 90 sec]**

- Week 1: "Complete Career Canvas and identify 3 target companies"
- Week 2: "Update resume with AI recommendations"

"Not sure what to plan? Click 'Generate with AI' and we'll suggest milestones based on your Career Canvas."

**[Sharing Your Plan - 15 sec]**
"Invite mentors or accountability partners to view your plan and provide feedback."

## VIDEO 5: Resume Analysis (3 min)

### Title: "AI-Powered Resume Analysis - Get Your ATS Score"

### Script Outline:

**[The ATS Problem - 30 sec]**
"75% of resumes never reach human recruiters - they're filtered by Applicant Tracking Systems. Our AI analyzes your resume the same way ATS systems do."

**[Uploading Your Resume - 30 sec]**
1. Navigate to Resume Analysis
2. Click "Upload Resume"
3. Select PDF file (max 10MB)
4. Wait for AI analysis (10-15 seconds)

**[Understanding Your Score - 60 sec]**
"Your ATS score ranges from 0-100:"
- Red (0-30): "Needs significant improvements"
- Yellow (31-70): "Good foundation, room to optimize"
- Green (71-100): "Excellent - you're ATS-ready"

**[Analysis Breakdown - 45 sec]**

*Strengths Tab:*
"What's working well - quantified achievements, relevant keywords, clean formatting."

*Gaps Tab:*
"What's missing - skills gaps, missing sections, weak areas."

*Recommendations Tab:*
"Specific, actionable improvements - add these keywords, restructure these sections."

**[Usage Limits - 15 sec]**
"Free accounts get 2 analyses per month. Pro users get 10. Your remaining quota is shown at the top."

## Production Notes

### Recording Setup
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (1080p)
- **Browser:** Chrome (latest)
- **Extensions:** Disable all
- **Demo Account:** Use clean account with realistic sample data
- **Cursor:** Use smooth, deliberate movements

### Audio Guidelines
- Clear, professional voiceover
- Consistent volume levels
- Background music (subtle, royalty-free)
- Sound effects for clicks (optional)

### Editing Guidelines
- Add lower-third text for key features
- Use zoom/highlight for important clicks
- Include chapter markers for longer videos
- Add end cards linking to next video

### Thumbnail Style
- Feature name in bold
- App screenshot in background
- Consistent brand colors
- Career Playbook logo

### Distribution Plan
- YouTube (main channel)
- Embed on app (Help section)
- Link from onboarding emails
- Social media teasers (30 sec clips)
